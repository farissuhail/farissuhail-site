Executive finding · 19 Jul 2026

  • The campaign is live, and the map is not what the pacts promised: nomination closed 18 Jul with 103 candidates for 36 seats. Only 11 seats are straight fights — Bersatu's 24 own-logo candidates produced 21 three-cornered contests plus two four-way and two five-way, resetting every projection built on a clean "BN–PN vs PH" map. Polling 1 Aug; early voting 28 Jul.
  • A constitutional dispute sits directly under the election: the unresolved Adat Perpatih conflict — the MB's April move against the Undang of Sungai Ujong, the Undang Yang Empat's counter-declaration against Tuanku Muhriz, and the subsequent withdrawal of support for MB Aminuddin by all 14 UMNO assemblymen — shapes the contest even as leaders across blocs insist it be kept out of it.
  • Rare cross-bloc boundary-setting — and the day's misinformation risk: Mohamad Hasan told BN candidates to leave the adat institution alone, Anwar urged all parties not to drag in the royal institution, and NS police warned against 3R content. Note this is a party-and-police boundary, not an Election Commission ruling — a distinction already being blurred online as "SPR larang isu adat".

Misinformation & verification register

⚠ "SPR larang isu adat" — MISATTRIBUTED, NEW. The instruction to keep the Adat Perpatih institution out of the campaign came from party leaders and police — Mohamad Hasan to BN candidates, Anwar to all parties, and the NS police chief on 3R. It is not an Election Commission ruling, and is circulating online as though it were. Attribute it correctly. ⚠ AI investment-scam wave — ACTIVE, HIGH. AI voice-clone and deepfake "testimoni" clips promising fast returns ("RM500 jadi RM2,000") continue to spread; treat any "untung cepat" testimony as a scam until verified. ⚠ N9 campaign 3R/fake-content risk — LIVE. The two-week campaign is now open: expect provocation, doctored "keputusan" content, and adat/royal framing despite the cross-bloc caution. Fuel: unsubsidised RON95/diesel genuinely rose this window (RM3.42 / RM4.07) — exaggerated "kenaikan mendadak" posters are the fake-price risk; only MOF/OpenDOSM figures are real; BUDI95 stays RM1.99/L. The @sebenarnya.my portal is the check-first reference.
  • ●Adat succession: the dispute is unresolved and contested — do not describe any removal or appointment as settled, in either direction. 3R-sensitive.
  • ●"SPR larang adat" — inaccurate; it was a party instruction plus a police caution, not an EC directive
  • ●Seat projections circulating as if official — none are; SPR issues no forecasts. The only official list is the SPR candidate list.
  • ●Bersatu remains inside PN while contesting on its own logo — do not report it as expelled or as a new coalition
  • ●Melaka: DAP left the state govt, but BN's 21/28 DUN seats mean the administration does NOT fall — don't report it as "kerajaan tumbang"
