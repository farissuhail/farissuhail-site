THE PROBLEM WE'RE HIRED FOR

Transformations rarely fail in the technology. They fail in the alignment.

The platform gets built. The consultants leave. And eighteen months later the organisation is running the old process on new software — over budget, behind schedule, with adoption nobody can prove. Not because anyone lacked effort, but because the four things that decide every transformation — people, process, technology and governance — were managed as four separate projects instead of one precision-engineered core.

Boards don't fund activity. They fund outcomes they can see, measure and keep. That is the standard this practice is built around.
