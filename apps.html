// Soalan lazim / FAQ

Questions, answered

How much does an app cost?

Three tiers: Starter from RM 8,000, Standard from RM 18,000, Pro from RM 35,000 — see the pricing section above for what's included in each. Every project gets a fixed quote upfront based on your exact scope. No surprises.

How long does it take?

A working MVP usually takes 2–6 weeks. A polished v1 ready for App Store and Google Play is typically 6–12 weeks, depending on scope.

Native or cross-platform — which should I pick?

Cross-platform (React Native / Flutter) gets you on iOS and Android faster and cheaper. Native is the right call when you need top performance or deep platform features. I'll recommend the right one in your quote.

Do you handle App Store and Google Play submission?

Yes — fully. Developer account setup, listing copy, icons, screenshots, privacy policy, review responses. You don't need to learn any of it.

What happens after the app launches?

I offer monthly support plans for updates, fixes, and new features. Or you can come back per-project whenever you need changes.

Do you work with businesses outside Malaysia?

Yes. I'm based in Kuala Lumpur (GMT+8) but work with clients across Southeast Asia and beyond, in English and Bahasa Malaysia.
